Meet Your Neighborhood Power Washer

A local teen getting his start in Preston Hollow

Hi! I'm just getting started in Preston Hollow

I'm 18, and I've lived in Preston Hollow for years. This neighborhood is home, and I genuinely love it here. I'm launching Preston Hollow Power Washing to help my neighbors keep their properties looking their best.

What started as cleaning up around my own home has grown into a real business: power washing driveways, patios, sidewalks, fences, and trash cans across the neighborhood. This is my first year, but Preston Hollow Power Washing is in its second season — and we run it as a two-person team, so you always get an extra set of hands and an extra eye for detail. I take pride in careful work and doing the job right.

When you hire me, you're not just getting clean surfaces. You're supporting a neighbor who genuinely loves this community and is excited to keep getting better at the craft.
